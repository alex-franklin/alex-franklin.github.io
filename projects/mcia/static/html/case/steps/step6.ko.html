Add Corrective Actions into the table below using the dropdown menus to make sure you have closed gaps that may have contributed to the incident. Note that you may place the Corrective Actions into as many rows as applicable, and you may place multiple Corrective Actions into any of the rows.

Steps to Follow:

  • Figure out which row(s) best describe(s) what the Corrective Action will do. Select the appropriate Corrective Action from the dropdown menu.
  • If the system had effective barriers in place before the incident occurred and there is no gap in the performance category, place a check in the “Barrier already in place...” column.
  • Review your analysis. Note that any performance category without a check or a Corrective Action is opening the incident to recurrence.
